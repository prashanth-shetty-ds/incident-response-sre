# On-Call Runbook

## Responsibilities
- Acknowledge alerts promptly
- Assess severity and impact
- Communicate status updates
- Coordinate mitigation efforts

## Escalation Triggers
- Customer impact
- Data loss risk
- SLA breach risk

## Principles
- Stabilize first, optimize later
- Prefer reversible actions
- Document actions during incidents

## Escalation
Escalation decisions are guided by the defined escalation policy.
On-call engineers are encouraged to escalate early when risk increases
or mitigation is unclear.
